    1. Optimize Review Management

    Given that 76% of customers are influenced by online reviews:

    • Implement systematic review collection post-procedure
    • Respond to all reviews within 24-48 hours
    • Feature reviews prominently on website and social media
    • Create video testimonials for higher engagement

    With 48.5% discovering through search:

    • Invest in SEO for medical tourism keywords
    • Create comprehensive content answering common questions
    • Optimize Google My Business profile
    • Develop location-specific landing pages

    3. Leverage Social Media Effectively

    Social media influences 43.6% of decisions:

    • Focus on Instagram (highest discovery rate at 10.7%)
    • Create before/after content series
    • Partner with micro-influencers in target markets
    • Develop TikTok strategy (growing from 1.8%)

    4. Strengthen Referral Program

    With 24.7% discovering through referrals:

    • Launch formal referral incentive program
    • Create shareable content for satisfied customers
    • Implement post-procedure follow-up for referral requests
    • Track and reward successful referrals

    5. Address the "Other" Category

    Based on analysis of 11.1% "Other" responses in decision factors:

    • Add "Customer Service Quality" as a tracking metric
    • Highlight "Before/After Results" more prominently
    • Emphasize "Trust & Professionalism" in messaging
    • Create "Value for Money" comparison content
